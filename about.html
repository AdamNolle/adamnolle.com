Hey! 👋

Thanks for taking the time to check out my website and learn a little about me. So first let me introduce myself. My name is Adam Nolle. I am currently a Senior at Missouri University of Science and Technology (or Missouri S&T). I am studying Computer Science and Computer Engineering. Since Spring of 2023 I have been the President of my school's ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) chapter. In my time as President I have brought back our annual MegaMiner event, helped to renovate the second floor CS lounge in our school’s computer Science building, and developed a Shopify merch shop and more!

(Content) Creator 🎨

I love creating things, specifically digital “content” online for people to enjoy and learn from. The place where I put most of my creative time is my YouTube channel which is linked in the nav bar above! I love making videos on things that catch my attention and have made it into a fun hobby for me to do.


On top of this I also have my blog “RAMdom Thought” where I just talked about anything that comes to mind that I think is interesting to either ponder upon or just talk about. I host my blog on Substack and if you’d liked you are more than welcome to subscribe for free!


Finally I also have a podcast! 🎧 For topics that I want to dive into a lot of detail into that I don’t think I convey in either a video or a blog post that is what my podcast is for. I don’t find talking for long periods of time to be too hard of a task anyways 😂. Also I figured if I had a YouTube channel and a blog I had to round it out with a podcast as well.
